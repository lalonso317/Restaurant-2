The Pub-Dawg Bar, was a dream come true for life-long friends Mike and Ryan. From there roots, being born and raised in Las Vegas, Nevada it shaped their view on life. They always saw the dark skies filled with flashing lights from what was a small strip. However, the Las Vegas Strip was only a small drive away from these kids, but a big dream. They wanted to be working in their city, the one that would givr them a lifetime of memories. It was until they grew into adults they realized the heart and soul of this city was in downtown. Graduating from UNLV, they took on the challenge and it was to open a bar. The trek to creating Pub-Dawg Bar was a long ride. In the opening, it will a triumph of in completing the this mission, but starting a new journey.
